PreDentTX does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes — ever.

What we collect

When you create an account or use PreDentTX, we collect the following information:

  • Your email address and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication)
  • Your role on the platform — predental student or dentist
  • Profile information you voluntarily provide: name, school, university, biography, and profile photo
  • For dentists: clinic name, address, specialty, shadowing requirements, and clinic photos
  • Shadowing requests, hour logs, event RSVPs, and job applications you submit
  • Resumes you upload when applying to positions

How we use your information

We use your information solely to operate the PreDentTX platform:

  • To connect predental students with dental clinics offering shadowing opportunities
  • To send email notifications about your requests, applications, and hour verifications
  • To display your profile to relevant parties (e.g., your student profile is visible to dentists you request from)
  • To maintain your hour log and track approved shadowing, volunteering, and paid hours
  • To improve the platform and fix technical issues

Who can see your information

PreDentTX is designed so that your data is only visible to people you directly interact with:

  • Dentist profiles and clinic listings are publicly visible to anyone who visits the site
  • Your student profile (name, school, bio, photo) is visible to dentists whose clinics you apply to or request from
  • Resumes you upload are accessible only to the dentist you submitted them to
  • Your shadowing requests and hour logs are only visible to you and the specific dentist involved
  • We do not display your email address publicly

Data storage and security

All data is stored in Google Firebase — a secure, SOC 2 compliant cloud platform. Passwords are never stored in plain text. Files and resumes are stored in Firebase Storage with access controls that restrict who can view them. We use industry-standard HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.

Email communications

PreDentTX sends transactional emails (via Resend) to notify you of activity on the platform — such as when a shadowing request is accepted or declined, or when your hours are verified. These are not marketing emails. You will only receive emails directly related to your activity on the platform.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw from the platform at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

Children's privacy

PreDentTX is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us so we can remove it promptly.
